About this Project

The eternal love between Radha and Krishna is the epitome of divine affection and devotion. It represents the ultimate union of the individual soul (Jivatma) with the Supreme Soul (Paramatma). This project is a small tribute to that divine love, created to help you share blessings, joy, and spiritual connection with friends and family through beautiful, personalized greeting cards. We hope this tool brings a little more light and love into your life.
